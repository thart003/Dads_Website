Leonard C. Hart Nibbrig has been practicing in Northern California since 1990 and in Sacramento since 1993. A native of Los Angeles, he graduated from U.C. Berkeley with High Honors in 1985. Mr. Hart Nibbrig earned his law degree three years later from University of Pennsylvania (Penn) Law School. His practice concentrates in civil litigation with approximately 30% of his cases in family law, 15% involving real estate disputes, and the remainder of his cases spanning contract actions, probate, and various other civil matters. He is a member in good standing with both the State Bar of California and the Sacramento County Bar Association, and was a member of the American Bar Association Advisory Panel (2001-2014). Mr. Hart Nibbrig raised his three adult offspring in Fair Oaks.
